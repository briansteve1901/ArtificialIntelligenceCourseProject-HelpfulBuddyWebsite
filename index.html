About Us


Helpful Buddy is your personal voice assistant with 2 features.

Helpful Buddy can play your favourite song and tell you time!


HOW TO USE


Tell Your Favourite Song to be Played

Do you feel boring or lonely? Just press Speak Now Button
and tell your favourite song to be played by saying "Play (The Song Name)
(The Song Singer) or just say "Play (The Song Name)".
Helpful Buddy will perfectly open YouTube and play your
favourite song.



Ask the Time

Do you want to know current time? Just press Speak
Now Button and ask "What time is it?" to Helpful Buddy.
Helpful Buddy will perfectly tell the current time!
